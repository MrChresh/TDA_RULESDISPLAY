Please make sure to read the rules as you will be held accountable if you break any.

Note: Certain rules are missing as they are not related to in-game content.

[2.1] FFA(Free-For-All) is not permitted.
●Unless you have a valid realistic roleplay reason to attack an another player, you are not allowed to.
●Examples of what valid reasons could be to attack an another player:
-Self Defence
-During a large-scale battle where battle is common
-You are a contracted killer and your victim is contracted.
-You are a member of a rival faction seeking to disrupt another organization. Authorization to attack a rival faction freely needs to be approved by an +Admin from the Staff Team first.
-Both parties consent
-Resist of arrest
-Tourneys
-Friendly Sparring
-Combat Event
-An ally is under attack
●Examples of what not valid reasons could be to attack an another player:
- Someone says "kill me." (for this, please find a secluded area to kill yourself. The port or an alleyway works good for this.)
- Insults.
- You are out-of-character.
- A sudden fit of rage.

[2.2] Metagaming
●Any information/data gathered from out-of-character means, such as third-party chat programs, or the global in-game chat, is to be voided(ignored).

▷Third Party Voice Chats are only allowed with players who are spawned in, or players who are not spawned in with players who are spawned in.

●Examples of Metagaming:
- Reading someone's nametag and acting as if your character knows their name or their history before they've introduced themselves to you.
 - Another player messages you out-of-character to tell you they're being robbed at a specific location, and you use this information to find them and help them.

[2.3] Powergaming
●Do not force your actions upon other players unless the player is under a situation in which you could.

[2.4] FearRP
●When you, or your party, is outnumbered/outgunned based on realistic circumstances, you are to act upon the instructions of the party that outnumbers/outguns you.

▷Outnumbered 1:3 would put you into FearRP.
●This includes situations in which you are also hindered from any ways of escape that are not fatal.

[2.5] ConsequenceRP
●ConsequenceRP occurs when you have to value the consequences of your action, this may come into act in such as Hostage Situations, or other situations.

▷Example: Your officer/friend has been kidnapped, you are to abide by the instructions of the kidnappers if applicable. Attacking them would put the officer/friend in fatal danger, in which you would disregard the consequences of such so.
●You should also consider the "End-Result Effects" of a situation. Seek example below.

▷Example: You're a hostage, and negotiations are failed. The person who captures you slashes your throat but he has to keep attacking you so you die in-game. In this situation, you are required to end your life if the attackers weren't able to finish you off.

[2.14] NLR(New Life Rule)
●Upon dying on any situation, except OOC, you may not return to the area of the place you died at for 15 minutes, or till the RP situation you were involved in is over.
●You cannot remember anything you gained from the situation that has resulted to your death, any related situations to the said one, and/or the past 20 minutes.
▷When you respawn, you will have to think of a relevant roleplay reason to not signify your character a CK. It could be such as "I've been knocked out", or so on.

●You will have to Roleplay your Injury for 15 minutes after you have respawned from your previous death.

▷This means that your combat would be restricted, and your physical attributes would not be fully potential, in other words, you would be fatigued.

▷An example of a limited physical attribute, is your ability to fight, jump for high heights, or a long stamina. Meaning you won't be able to fight, jump from high heights, or you would be fatigued.

[2.15] Contracted Kills(Assassinations)
● In order to have a contract to kill an individual, the person that hires you is required to have a roleplay reason to do so.

▷Examples of reasons to make a contract kill on an individual could be seen at [2.1] FFA(Free-for-All) rule's details.

You are required to capture a photo, video, game rec, etc, of anyone hiring you. Details that must be included:

-Who is the victim(Person to be killed)?
-Reason?(This info could be done OOCly)
-The image must show the identity of the person whose making the contract.

●If you manage to kill the individual that was targeted in the contract, you are to announce in the global/OOC chat the following details:

-Inform the attack was due to a contract killing
-Reason
-Optionally, the name of hte person who called the contract. The person has to approve first.

●You must actively attempt to complete the contract.

[2.16] Character Kills(CKs)

●CKs may be approved through the #ticket-creation system from a Staff Vote.

▷A valid reason is required for a CK.

●A CK will permanently kill your character, thus resetting your inventory, lands, and any other details.
●During the active phase the target is CK-able, the target may CK you back if you failed to eliminate him.
●Character-Kills(CKs) may occur due to contracted kills, executions, and/or war-time events.

[2.17] TortureRP
● Any Torture roleplay should not consist of extreme or gruesome details/actions unless authorized by the victim.

▷Examples include:
- Teeth pulling - Minor
- Dagger incisions - Minor
- Bolts in kneecaps - Minor
- Bone Breaking - Minor
- Dismemberments - Minor but you should still ask permission if you want it PERMANENT
- Vein Splitting/Cutting - Extreme
- Ligament Twisting - Extreme
- Skinning - Extreme
